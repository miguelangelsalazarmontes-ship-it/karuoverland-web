Our Plans & Experiences

Every corner of the Cordillera Blanca awaits you. Packages designed for those who want to enjoy without complications.

Sunrise at Lake Radián

🕐 Extended Full Day · 🐾 Moderate Difficulty

"First light in the Andes". Ideal for photography, nature lovers, and acclimatization hikes.

Night in Domes – Lake Radián

🕐 2 days / 1 night · 🐾 Moderate Difficulty

"Sunset, stars, and sunrise". A photographic and intimate experience under the Andean sky.

Sunrise in Wilcacocha

🕐 Half day · 🐾 Easy to Moderate

Birdwatching: "Waking up among mountains and Andean songs". Great for families and nature.

Lake Parón Full Day

🕐 Full day · 🐾 Moderate Difficulty

"Turquoise among ice giants". Short altitude hike for landscape and photography lovers.

Llanganuco Lakes

🕐 Full day · 🐾 Easy

"Between mountains, forests, and emerald waters". A relaxing experience in Huascarán National Park.

Chacas & Punta Olímpica

🕐 Full day · 🐾 Easy

"From the Cordillera Blanca to the artisan heart of Conchucos". Altitude, tradition, and crafts.

City Tour Huaraz + Palestra

🕐 Extended half day · 🐾 Easy to Moderate

"City, community, and vertical adventure". Urban culture, history, and rock climbing.

City Tour + ATVs

🕐 Extended half day · 🐾 Easy

"City, altitude, and adventure". Discover Huaraz culturally and finish with ATVs in Pukaventana.
